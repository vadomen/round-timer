# Privacy Policy for RoundTimer

**Last updated: December 26, 2025**

Vadim Pospelov ("we," "our," or "us") operates the RoundTimer mobile application (the "Service").

## 1. No Data Collection
RoundTimer is designed with privacy in mind. We **do not collect, store, or share any personal information** or usage data from our users.

- **No Personal Data**: We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personal identifiers.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use any cookies, tracking pixels, or 3rd party analytics.
- **No Cloud Storage**: All timer settings and workout data (if any) are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

## 2. Permissions
To provide the best experience, the app may request the following permissions:
- **Bluetooth**: Only used to connect to your local Heart Rate Monitor (if you choose to use that feature). No heart rate data is transmitted over the internet.
- **Audio/Notifications**: Used to play the round bells and alerts.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
